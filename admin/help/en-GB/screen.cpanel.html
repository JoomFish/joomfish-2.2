Control Panel

Introduction

The Joom!Fish Control Panel is the primary dialog from which you can work through the process of translating your site. Beside the links to the Joom!Fish features it also includes general information about the Joom!Fish project, the state of your extension including configuration settings and an overview of unpublished translations.
